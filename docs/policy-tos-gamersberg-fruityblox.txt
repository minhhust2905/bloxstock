Cookies
FruityBlox.com uses cookies to enhance your user experience and track website performance. Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help us remember your preferences, analyze website usage, and personalize content and ads.

We use two types of cookies on our website:

- Session cookies: These cookies are temporary and are automatically deleted when you close your browser. They are used to maintain your session as you navigate through the website.

- Persistent cookies: These cookies remain on your device until they expire or you manually delete them. They are used to remember your preferences and settings for future visits to our website.

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, but doing so may impact the functionality and features of our website. By using FruityBlox.com, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
