📦 Information We Collect

Usage Data: Like most online services, we use third-party tools such as Google Analytics to collect anonymous information about how you interact with BloxStock. This includes your IP address (anonymized), browser type, device information, and pages visited. This data helps us improve the user experience and service performance.

Local Storage: To provide a fast experience, BloxStock stores a temporary cache of stock data in your browser's localStorage. This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Server Logs: Our hosting infrastructure (Cloudflare) automatically records basic technical logs required for security and service stability.

🍪 Cookies & Advertising

BloxStock and our third-party partners (such as Google AdSense) use cookies and similar technologies to analyze site traffic, personalize content, and serve relevant advertisements. These technologies allow us to maintain BloxStock as a free service for the community.

You can manage or opt-out of cookie tracking through your browser settings or by using specialized privacy tools.

🛡️ Data Sharing & Security

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with trusted service providers (like Cloudflare or Google) to the extent necessary to operate our website and analyze our traffic. We implement reasonable technical safeguards to protect any data processed by our systems.

Our service contains links to external sites like Roblox. BloxStock is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party websites.
