📦 What Data We Collect

Local Storage (Browser-side only): BFStock stores a temporary cache of stock data in your browser's localStorage. This is used purely to speed up page load times and is never sent to any server. You can clear this at any time via your browser settings.

Server Logs: Like all websites, our hosting provider (Cloudflare Pages) may automatically record basic access logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited). These logs are managed by Cloudflare and are not accessible to or processed by us for tracking purposes.

🍪 Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and local storage to enhance your experience, analyze our traffic, and potentially serve relevant advertisements via partners like Google AdSense. These technologies help us understand how you use the site and allow us to keep BFStock free for everyone.

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings.

🔗 External Links
