About BloxStock

The story behind the tracker you use every day.

👋 Hi, I'm Minh Edward

I'm a passionate Blox Fruits player and developer who built BloxStock because I was tired of manually checking the in-game shop and missing rare fruits like Kitsune and Dragon.

BloxStock was created as a personal side project to solve a real problem for the community — and it has since grown into a tool used by thousands of players every day.

🎯 What is BloxStock?

BloxStock is a real-time, fan-made stock tracker for the Blox Fruits game on Roblox. Our system automatically aggregates and synchronizes the latest Devil Fruits data from:

📦 Normal Dealer — Verified and updated every 4 hours.

🌌 Advanced Dealer (Mirage Island) — Verified and updated every 2 hours.

It also keeps a full history log of all past rotations, processed and stored within our internal database for community analysis.

🛡️ Transparency

BloxStock is a fan-made tool created for the community. It is not affiliated with Gamer Robot Inc. or Roblox Corporation. All game assets are used for informational purposes and remain the property of their respective owners.

Currently, BloxStock is ad-free to prioritize user experience, but we may use standard advertising and analytics in the future to maintain our infrastructure.
