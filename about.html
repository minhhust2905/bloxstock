About BFStock

The story behind the tracker you use every day.

👋 Hi, I'm Minh Edward

I'm a passionate Blox Fruits player and developer who built BFStock because I was tired of manually checking the in-game shop and missing rare fruits like Kitsune and Dragon.

BFStock was created as a personal side project to solve a real problem for the community — and it has since grown into a tool used by thousands of players every day.

🎯 What is BFStock?

BFStock is a real-time, fan-made stock tracker for the Blox Fruits game on Roblox. It automatically fetches and displays the current Devil Fruits available from:

📦 Normal Dealer — Updates every 4 hours.

🌌 Advanced Dealer (Mirage Island) — Updates every 2 hours.

It also keeps a full history log of all past rotations so you can track patterns and predict when your favorite fruit will appear next.

🛡️ Transparency

BFStock is a fan-made tool created for the community. It is not affiliated with Gamer Robot Inc. or Roblox Corporation. All game assets are used for informational purposes and remain the property of their respective owners.

Currently, BFStock is ad-free to prioritize user experience, but we may use standard advertising and analytics in the future to maintain our infrastructure.
